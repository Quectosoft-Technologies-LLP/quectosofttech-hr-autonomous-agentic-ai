# Code of Conduct

**Organisation:** Quectosoft Technologies LLP  
**Maintained by:** Subrit Dikshit · subrit@quectosofttech.com · subrit@gmail.com

---

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming, inclusive, safe, and harassment-free space for contributors, operators, researchers, developers, and enterprise adopters of all backgrounds, identities, disciplines, and experience levels.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism professionally.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.
- Showing empathy toward other contributors and users.
- Participating in reviews and discussions in good faith.

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Harassment, trolling, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Discriminatory language or behaviour based on personal characteristics.
- Unsolicited commercial spam or repeated self-promotion.
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in issues, pull requests, reviews, or discussions.
- Bad-faith reporting, brigading, or attempts to derail collaboration.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to: **subrit@quectosofttech.com**

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and fairly. Reporters will not face retaliation for making a good-faith report.

Consequences may range from a warning to temporary restriction or permanent ban, depending on severity, repetition, and impact.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.
- Project documentation and communication channels.
- Community spaces where individuals represent the project.
